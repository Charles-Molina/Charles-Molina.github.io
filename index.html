Social Change Project: Make-a-Wish Fundraiser:

During this project I was part of a small team whose objective was to raise awareness and collect donations for the Make-a-Wish foundation, we were assigned a young girl named Christine who was dealing with Pancreatic cancer at only 6 years old. It became clear to us that we had a responsibility to help this young girl in need by raising the necessary funds and making her wish come true. We collaborated as a group and leveraged digital platforms such as social media, and collection sites like GoFundMe to help make this all possible. In the end not only did Christine beat her battle with cancer, but we were able to play our part and raise well over our initial goals and we were able to make Christine's wish come true!
